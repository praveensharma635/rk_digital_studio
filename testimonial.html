Terms & Conditions

1. Booking & Payment

A 50% advance payment is required to confirm any booking. Remaining balance must be paid on or before the day of the shoot.
Bookings are subject to availability and are confirmed only after receipt of advance payment.

2. Cancellation & Refund Policy

Bookings cancelled 7 days before the event will receive a 50% refund of the advance.
